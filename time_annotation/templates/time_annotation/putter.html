You are going to separate a part of novel as a temporal block. You can think of a part of given text as a edge of a block if
  • flow of the story go back to the past
  • or when the flow of the story leaps with huge time gap

    • You can make a block by
  • dragging on the part of the text you want to make as a block on the left pane
  • writing summary of the part and submit on the right pane

    • if you want to make revision, or revert the part you made, you can simply put your mouse on the summary and then click buttons If you made the whole text as temporal blocks, then you will see submission dialogue. Even if you do not submit rightly, you can submit anytime by clicking End Task button on the left bottom.
